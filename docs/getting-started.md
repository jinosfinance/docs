# Getting Started with JinOS
This guide walks you through signing in, setting up your wallet, and issuing your first virtual card.
---
## Prerequisites
- A valid email address
- A modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, Safari)
- No crypto wallet extension required — JinOS creates one for you
---
## Step 1 — Sign In
1. Go to **https://jinos.xyz**
2. Click **Sign In**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for a one-time login code
5. Enter the code on the JinOS login screen
> JinOS uses **Privy** for authentication. Privy creates a secure MPC (Multi-Party Computation) embedded wallet on your behalf — no seed phrase to save, no MetaMask required.
---
## Step 2 — Your Embedded BSC Wallet
Once signed in, JinOS automatically provisions you a **BNB Smart Chain (BSC)** wallet:
- Your wallet address is visible on the **Wallet** page
- This is a real BSC wallet — it works on BSC Mainnet (Chain ID: 56)
- You fully own this wallet. JinOS never has access to the private key
- To fund your wallet, send BNB or USDT to your displayed address from any exchange or external wallet
**Important:** This is a BSC Mainnet wallet. Only send BSC-compatible tokens (BEP-20).
---
## Step 3 — Dashboard Overview
After signing in, you land on the **Dashboard**:
| Section | Description |
|---|---|
| Total Balance | Your combined wallet balance in USD |
| BSC Network | Live block number and gas price |
| Top Movers | Live price data for BNB, BTC, ETH, CAKE |
| Recent Activity | Your latest card transactions |
---
## Step 4 — Issue a Virtual Card
1. Go to the **Card** page from the taskbar
2. Click **Issue New Card**
3. Enter a label (e.g. "Subscriptions", "Shopping")
4. Click **Create Card**
Your card is issued instantly with:
- A unique 16-digit virtual card number
- Expiry date (3 years from issue)
- CVV
- $0.00 starting balance
### Fund Your Card
1. Click on your card to open the detail view
2. Click **Fund Card**
3. Enter the USD amount to fund
4. Confirm — funds move from your wallet balance to the card
### Use Your Card
Use the card number, expiry, and CVV anywhere Visa virtual cards are accepted online.
---
## Step 5 — AI Agent
Go to the **Agent** page to configure your AI financial assistant:
- **Chat** — Ask the agent anything: "What is my biggest spending category?" or "Rebalance my portfolio to 60% BNB."
- **Tasks** — Enable automated tasks:
  - Auto-rebalance portfolio
  - Yield optimization
  - Spend monitoring alerts
  - Auto-top-up card when balance is low
---
## Step 6 — Portfolio Tracking
Go to **Portfolio** to see:
- Total USD value across all your BSC tokens
- 24h change in USD and percentage
- Historical performance chart
- Asset allocation breakdown (BNB, USDT, USDC, CAKE, etc.)
---
## Step 7 — Transaction History
Go to **Transactions** to see your full on-chain history:
- Filter by: All, Send, Receive, Swap, Stake
- Each row shows: type, transaction hash, date, amount, USD value
---
